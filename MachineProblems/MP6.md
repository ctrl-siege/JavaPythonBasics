## Machine Problem 6

Create a program that accepts 10 student grades. It should display:

- The average grade
- The highest grade
- The lowest grade
- The number of students who passed (passing grade = 75)

Constraints: Do NOT use built-in min/max functions.

Sample input:
```
82
76
91
68
74
88
95
79
83
70
```

Sample output:
```
Average Grade: 80
Highest Grade: 95
Lowest Grade: 68
Number of Students Passed: 7
 
```
