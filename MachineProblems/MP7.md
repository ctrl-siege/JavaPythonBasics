## Machine Problem 7

Input 15 integers into an array. It should display:

- Count of even numbers
- Count of odd numbers
- Count of positive numbers
- Count of negative numbers

Constraits: 0 is neither a positive or negative number.

Sample input:
```
3
-4
6
9
12
-7
5
8
-2
14
1
-9
10
11
-6
```

Sample output:
```
Count of Even Numbers: 8
Count of Odd Numbers: 7
Count of Positive Numbers: 10
Count of Negative Numbers: 5
```
